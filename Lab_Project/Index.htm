Welcome To This Application!

Hello, my name is Nicholas Spataro and this is my webpage for my Moving Planner application! This web application is designed to walk you through the process of planning for a move by assisting in the scheduling, company choosing, supply obtaining, and the repairs and cleaning processes of packing up your home and moving to another location.

Instructions

This is a simple application and walks you through each and every step of your move.
Please click the instruction tabe and then click the "Next" and "Previous" buttons accordingly to navigate through the module.
You may also return home at any point.
