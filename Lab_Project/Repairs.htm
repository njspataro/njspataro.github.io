For Repairs and Cleaning:

Prior to moving out of a home or rental property, you are going to want to complete some quick little repairs, as well as give the place a nice clean sweep.
Some of these repairs and cleaning tasks are covered by the rental property agreement but some of them cost you money!
Common repairs people can do before moving out of a rental home:

  • Fill in holes in the walls where pictures or decorations once hung with plaster
  • Replace burnt out light bulbs
  • Paint over scuffs on the walls
  • Repair scratches in wood or tile flooring
  • Replace air filters in HVAC/furnace

Review with your landlord or lease agreement to find out the repairs that are coved by you prior to moving out.
Click the "Cleaning List" button to find common cleaning tasks that can be included on your lease agreement.
Cleaning List

    Cleaning List

  1. Vaccum carpet and/or sweep & mop floors.
  2. Wipe down all flat surfaces.
  3. Wipe down all kitchen cabinets and drawers.
  4. Clean appliances such as stove, oven, microwave, and refridgerator.
  5. Clean all bathrooms.
  6. Remove all belongings and trash from residence
  7. Clean outside residence ( Mow grass if applicable, etc...)
